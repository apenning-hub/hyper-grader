Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

TL;DR — The short version

  • All your data is stored locally on your Mac
  • Speech recognition runs on-device — recordings never leave your computer
  • No tracking, analytics, or telemetry of any kind
  • If you use Claude API, data is sent to Anthropic only when you click Generate
  • Your API key is stored in macOS Keychain, not by us
  • We don't operate any servers. Your data is yours.

Introduction

Hyper Grader is designed with your privacy as a first principle. This policy explains exactly what the app does — and doesn't do — with your data.

Data Storage

Local only

All courses, assessments, rubrics, grading records, and student names are stored locally on your Mac in the standard macOS application container. None of this data is uploaded to any server, because we don't operate any servers.

Speech recognition

Voice transcription is performed entirely on-device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework. Voice recordings are processed locally and never leave your Mac. Transcripts are stored with your grading records, locally.

API key storage

If you use the Claude API integration, your API key is stored in macOS Keychain using system-level encryption. We never see your key.

Optional Third-Party Services

Claude API (optional)

If you choose to generate feedback using the Claude API, when you click "Generate" the following is sent to Anthropic's servers:

  • Rubric criteria and your ratings
  • Voice note transcripts (if any)
  • Student name (if you've entered one)
  • Your feedback style configuration

This data is used solely to generate feedback. You control when this happens — data is only sent when you explicitly click Generate with Claude API selected. Anthropic's privacy policy governs this transmission: anthropic.com/privacy.

On-device AI (alternative)

Hyper Grader supports Apple Intelligence as an alternative to the Claude API. When using on-device AI, no data leaves your Mac at all.

File Import / Export

When importing rubrics (e.g. from Canvas CSV) or exporting grade reports, all processing is local. Files are read from and written to locations you choose via the standard macOS file picker. No data is uploaded.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following on our end:

  • Student names or personal information (these stay on your device only)
  • Grading data or feedback content
  • Voice recordings or transcripts
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports (only shared with Apple if you opt in via TestFlight settings)
  • Your location
  • Device identifiers

Student Data & FERPA

When you enter student names in the app, that data stays on your device. You are responsible for complying with applicable student privacy laws (FERPA, COPPA, GDPR, etc.) in your jurisdiction. If you use the Claude API, consider whether student names need to be included — the app works without them.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, share, rent, or trade your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes. Since we do not operate any servers and collect no data ourselves, there is nothing to sell. This applies to all users, including California residents under the CCPA.

Children's Privacy

The app is designed for educators and is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum digital age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Your Control Over Your Data

  • Access: All data is in the app's standard macOS container (accessible via Finder)
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Export: Export grading records to CSV at any time from within the app
  • Questions: For any data-related requests or questions, email me@apenning.is

GDPR — European Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Because Hyper Grader does not collect or process your personal data on any server, the standard GDPR rights — access, rectification, erasure, portability, and restriction of processing — are exercised directly within the app:

  • Access & Export: Use the CSV export feature within the app
  • Delete: Remove individual records in the app or uninstall to erase all data
  • Questions: Contact me@apenning.is

The only third-party processing occurs when you explicitly choose to use the Claude API (see above). In that case, Anthropic acts as a data processor under their own GDPR terms. You retain full control over whether and when that transmission occurs.

Changes to This Policy
