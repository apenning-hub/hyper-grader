What it does

🎙️

Voice-powered grading

Record voice notes while reviewing student work. On-device speech recognition transcribes everything in real time — no typing required.

✨

AI-generated feedback

Choose between on-device Apple Intelligence or Claude API. Consistent, rubric-aligned feedback personalised for each student.

📋

Flexible rubric management

Create custom rubrics from scratch or import directly from Canvas CSV exports. Reuse across courses and terms.

📊

Export to your gradebook

Export grades and feedback to CSV in one click. Works with any gradebook system that accepts spreadsheets.

🔒

Privacy-first design

All grading data stays on your Mac. Speech recognition runs on-device. You decide if and when anything leaves your computer.

⚡

Built for speed

Keyboard shortcuts to move between students, batch student queues, consistency checker, and time-per-student tracking.

🛡️

Your data never leaves your Mac (unless you want it to)

No accounts. No servers. No telemetry. Your students' data belongs to you and stays on your device. The only optional exception is generating feedback via the Claude API — and you control exactly when that happens.

✓ Local storage only ✓ On-device speech recognition ✓ No analytics ✓ No tracking ✓ API key in Keychain ✓ FERPA-friendly

From spoken notes to student feedback in seconds

A workflow designed to fit how you already grade — not replace it.

1

Set up your course and rubric

Create a course and assessment in the sidebar. Import your rubric from Canvas as a CSV, or build one from scratch. Takes under two minutes.

2

Select ratings and record your notes

Click the rubric ratings for each criterion, then hit Record and speak naturally. Your words are transcribed on-device as you talk.

3

Generate and copy feedback

Hit Generate. AI turns your raw notes and ratings into a polished, professional comment. Copy it, move to the next student with ⌘→.

4

Export when you're done

All grades and feedback are saved automatically. When the batch is finished, export to CSV and paste into your gradebook.
